### 22-23 November 2022

Topics include (but are not restricted to) multi-physics coupling
problems, interface problems, multi-domain problems, preconditioning
of high frequency scattering, multi-methods coupling (such as
FEM/BEM).


#### Speakers

- Silvia Bertoluzza
- Erik Burman
- Miguel Fernandez
- Christophe Geuzaine
- Mats Larson
- Frederic Nataf
- Olaf Steinbach
